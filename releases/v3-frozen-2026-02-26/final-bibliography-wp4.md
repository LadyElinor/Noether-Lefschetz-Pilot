## Final Bibliography (Annotated for WP4)

- **Voisin, C. (2003).** *Hodge Theory and Complex Algebraic Geometry II*. Cambridge University Press.  
  **Relevance:** Section 3.3 (Noether–Lefschetz theorem context) for density/codimension framing of special loci.

- **Huybrechts, D. (2016).** *Lectures on K3 Surfaces*. Cambridge University Press.  
  **Relevance:** Chapter 1 (basic K3 intersection framework) and Chapter 11 (Picard group context) for geometric background behind divisor/incidence interpretation.

- **Hartshorne, R. (1977).** *Algebraic Geometry*. Springer-Verlag.  
  **Relevance:** Section III.5 (Picard group) and Section V.1 (Adjunction formula) for adjunction-style genus-consistency framing in Tier 3 (heuristic use).

- **Wilson, E. B. (1927).** “Probable inference, the law of succession, and statistical inference.” *Journal of the American Statistical Association*.  
  **Relevance:** Wilson score interval used for sparse-proportion confidence ceilings (e.g., Batch-L upper bound reporting).
